Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Luntra Outreach ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered lead generation and outreach service (the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, company details
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through Stripe
  • Lead Data: Contact information you upload or search for
  • Email Content: Templates and messages you create
  • Communication: Support requests and feedback

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our Service
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, operating system
  • Cookies: Session data and preferences
  • Log Data: Server logs and error reports

2.3 Third-Party Data

  • Lead Sources: Publicly available business information from Apollo.io
  • Email Delivery: Delivery metrics from Resend/SendGrid

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Provide lead generation and email outreach services
  • Account Management: Create and manage your account
  • Payment Processing: Handle billing and subscriptions
  • AI Personalization: Generate personalized email content
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage to enhance our platform
  • Support: Respond to inquiries and provide assistance
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal and regulatory requirements
  • Security: Protect against fraud and abuse

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Payment Processing: Stripe for secure payment handling
  • Email Delivery: Resend or SendGrid for email sending
  • Data Sources: Apollo.io for lead enrichment
  • AI Services: OpenAI for content personalization
  • Infrastructure: Netlify for hosting and deployment

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process or government requests
  • Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Investigate potential violations

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and monitoring
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard cloud security

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Data: Until account deletion plus 30 days
  • Lead Data: Until you remove it or account deletion
  • Email Logs: 90 days for delivery tracking
  • Payment Records: As required by law (typically 7 years)
  • Usage Analytics: 2 years in aggregated form

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • Request copies of your personal data
  • Export your lead data and email templates

7.2 Correction and Deletion

  • Update or correct your account information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Remove specific lead records

7.3 Control and Consent

  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Manage cookie preferences
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. International Data Transfers

Our Service is hosted in the United States. If you are accessing our Service from outside the US, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US where our servers are located and our service providers operate.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

11. European Privacy Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your personal data
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing

12. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze how our Service is used
  • Provide personalized content
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect Service functionality.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending email notification for material changes

Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
